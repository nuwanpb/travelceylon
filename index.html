Explore the Island

Top Destinations We Cover

From misty mountains to golden beaches, ancient ruins to wild safari parks — Sri Lanka has it all.

Ancient Heritage
Sigiriya — Lion Rock

The iconic 5th century rock fortress rising 200m above the jungle. A UNESCO World Heritage wonder.

Hill Country
Ella

Nine Arches Bridge, misty peaks and famous tea trails.

Beautiful Beaches
Mirissa & Unawatuna

Golden sands, whale watching, and ocean sunsets.

Cultural Capital
Kandy

Temple of the Sacred Tooth, Kandy Lake, and Perahera festivals.

Tea Country
Nuwara Eliya

Lush tea estates, waterfalls and cool mountain air.

Colonial Heritage
Galle Fort

A Dutch colonial fortress with cobblestone streets and ocean views.

Wildlife Safari
Yala National Park

Sri Lanka's leopards, elephants, and exotic birdlife.

Ancient City
Anuradhapura

2,500-year-old sacred Buddhist capital and giant stupas.

Ancient City
Polonnaruwa

Medieval ruins of the second great kingdom of Sri Lanka.

City & Gateway
Colombo

Sri Lanka's vibrant capital — markets, temples, food, and nightlife.
